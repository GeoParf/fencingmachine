Types of signal

There are 7 types of signals in the application. The first four increase the number of different signals from one to four. The signals will alternate in a random order after a random period of time. The next three signal types contain two signals in a row, the first is constant, and the second can change depending on the selected type.

Each type of signal corresponds to a color and sound indicator.

You can associate the signal with a specific movement. For example, when the signal turns red, you need to lunge during a footwork then recover and continue the footwork until the next signal.

When the exercise time is over, the application will give you a double sound signal and the “Strat” button will turn green.

Exercise adjustments

You can click on to change the exercise settings such as:

Duration of the exercise;

The number of repetitions during the exercise;

The maximum time between signals.
